**Date:** October 26, 2023

## Executive Summary

This report analyzes the performance of Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns between January 1, 2025, and March 31, 2025. Overall, the campaign achieved a healthy ROAS of 5.83.  However, significant opportunities exist to improve performance through strategic adjustments to audience targeting, creative optimization, and platform allocation. Key recommendations include increasing investment in the retargeting audience and the US market, pausing underperforming creatives, reallocating budget to Facebook, and refreshing creatives to combat fatigue.

*   **Facebook vs. Instagram:** Facebook slightly outperformed Instagram with a ROAS of 9.90 compared to 9.38.

## 9. Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

1.  **Increase Retargeting Budget:** Allocate a larger portion of the budget to the retargeting audience to capitalize on its high ROAS.
2.  **Refresh Creatives:** Replace or refresh creatives with high impressions and low ROAS to combat creative fatigue. Focus on replacing those with over 1M impressions and a ROAS below 20.
3.  **Increase US Market Investment:** Increase budget allocation to the US market to leverage its strong performance.
4.  **Pause Underperforming Creatives:** Immediately pause or replace creatives with a ROAS below 3.
5.  **Reallocate Budget to Facebook:** Shift a portion of the budget from Instagram to Facebook to capitalize on Facebook's higher ROAS.
6.  **Stabilize Campaign Settings:** Implement more stable campaign settings (e.g., budget pacing, bid strategies) to reduce daily ROAS fluctuations.
7.  **Scale High-Performing Creative:** Increase budget or broaden the target audience for the creative "Doctors recommend breathable modal — meet our men boxers."
8.  **Ongoing Monitoring:** Continuously monitor performance metrics (ROAS, CTR, CPM, CPC) and adjust strategies as needed.

## 10. Conclusion

By implementing these recommendations, the overall ROAS and efficiency of the Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns can be significantly improved. A focus on data-driven decision-making and continuous optimization will be critical for sustained success.
