## Actionable Insights and Recommendations

Based on the performance analysis, the following actionable insights and recommendations are provided:

*   **Retargeting Audience Optimization:** Increase budget allocation to retargeting audiences by 20% to capitalize on their high ROAS.
*   **Creative Refresh:** Replace the bottom 20% performing creatives with new creatives that highlight different product features to combat creative fatigue.
*   **US Market Focus:** Shift 15% of the budget from IN and UK to the US market to leverage its higher ROAS potential.
*   **Broad Audience Refinement:** Implement interest-based targeting within the broad audience segment, focusing on 'active lifestyle' and 'comfort clothing' to improve performance.
*   **Pause Underperforming Creatives:** Pause creatives with a ROAS below 3 and replace them with variations focusing on customer testimonials.
*   **Facebook Optimization:** Allocate 10% more budget to Facebook from Instagram, monitor ROAS and evaluate impacts.
