# ROAS Drop and Creative Fatigue Analysis

## Summary

Overall ROAS has decreased, likely due to creative fatigue. Performance varies significantly across different creatives, suggesting a need for creative refresh and focusing on top-performing themes. The overall ROAS is 5.83, but daily ROAS has declined from 11.22 on 2025-01-02 to 6.92 on 2025-03-31.

## Key Insights

*   **ROAS Decline:** ROAS has declined significantly over time.
*   **Creative Fatigue:** Some creatives show low ROAS and CTR, indicating fatigue.
*   **Performance Variance:** A large gap exists between the performance of top and bottom creatives.
